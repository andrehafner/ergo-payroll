    Header Definitions: Leave cell blank if you don't use it, do NOT remove column!

    company
    Your Company Name

    company_wallet_id (whatever you put in will be overridden by your login wallet, this is intentional for now).
    The ERGO wallet you will be paying from

    employee_wallet_id
    Your employee's ERGO receiving address

    employee_email
    Optional: Your employee's email for notifications

    employee_token_id
    The token id you want to pay your employee in

    employee_token_count
    How many of those tokes (do not use if you are entering a USD amount in the next column

    employee_usd_amount
    Alternate: Pay them in the chosen token but in a set USD amount (do not use if you use the previous column)

    employee_pay_frequency
    How many times per year to pay employee (24,26,52 are the only options)

    employee_active_status
    yes or no
